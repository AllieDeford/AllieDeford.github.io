Advising Planning Program


The Advising Planning Program (APP) was Allie's senior capstone project at the University of Evansville. The project was completed during the 2013-2014 school year. Allie and her partner, Tyler, were tasked with creating an application to help student at the University plan schedules and keep track of graduation requirements. The aim of the system was to replace the current tracking method, which consisted to paper checklists kept by the student's advisor.

Allie was specifically tasked with creating the user interface for this system. She completed a series of interviews, and used on that information to create a list of nessesary features. She then set about prototyping two seperate interfaces using HTML/CSS/Javascript. She tested these two interfaces with a usability study. Finally, she used the results of the study to complete a final prototype.

APP was presented twice: first at MESCON 2014 and again during the EECS senior project presentation session. For her work, Allie was awarded the Most Professionally Developed senior project. For more information, please see the links below.
